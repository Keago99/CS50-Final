How to lift! Exercise instructional videos

Squat

Known as the king of lower body exercises, it increases total lower body strength and flexibility, a must have for a truly strong individual.

Deadlift

Known as the King of exercises, great for a strong posterior chain and grip, nothing says strong like simply picking up a heavy object.

Bench

The most popular exercise with good reason, nothing challenges the upper body pressing musculature in such a stable position

Overhead Press

A classic strongman exercise, nothing shows real world strength better than extending a weight fully overhead, creates a strong core, wrists and traps.

Barbell Row

A classic back builder that allows you to move heavy weights while sparing your lower back, ensure you are controlling the weight through the range of motion and not heaving.

Pull-up

The Pull-up is great for overall upper body musculature and helps even out development so we do not become tight due to all the pressing in our program.

Assisted Pull-up

Assisted Pull-ups are a great stepping stone to a ful fledged pull-up, patience and control are key to growing the strength and co-ordination to excute the movement un-assisted.

Barbell Curl

A classic to build some big biceps and help balance out the forces on the shoulder and elbow, no point being strong if you still have small arms.
